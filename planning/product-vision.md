## 1. Vision Statement

### The Elevator Pitch
**FOR** university students and young adults **WHO** struggle to maintain consistent healthy habits due to busy academic schedules and stress, **THE** VitaTrack **IS A** personal health monitoring application **THAT** provides intuitive daily logging, visual progress analytics, and personalized wellness insights. **UNLIKE** generic fitness apps (such as MyFitnessPal or Apple Health) that focus primarily on calories and step counting, **OUR PRODUCT** addresses holistic wellness including mental health, sleep quality, and stress management specifically tailored for student lifestyles, helping users discover patterns and make informed decisions about their physical and mental well-being.

| Characteristic | Description |

| **Goals** | Create sustainable healthy habits, monitor wellness trends |

### Tertiary Users: Healthcare Providers
| Characteristic | Description |
|----------------|-------------|
| **Needs** | Access patient health history, identify trends |
| **Pain Points** | Limited consultation time, incomplete patient self-reporting |
| **Goals** | Provide data-informed recommendations |

## 3. Product Goals

### Primary Goals
1. **Enable Easy Daily Tracking** - Users can log health metrics in under 30 seconds
2. **Provide Visual Insights** - Clear charts showing health trends over time
3. **Support Goal Setting** - Allow users to set and track personalized health targets
4. **Promote Mental Health Awareness** - Include mood and stress tracking features

### Secondary Goals
5. **Facilitate Data Sharing** - Export health reports for medical consultations
6. **Send Smart Reminders** - Gentle notifications to maintain tracking habits
7. **Ensure Data Privacy** - Secure storage of sensitive health information

## 7. Constraints & Assumptions

### Constraints
- 4-week development timeline
- 5-person team with limited resources
- Prototype scope (not full production app)
- Must demonstrate Agile/Scrum methodology

### Assumptions
- Users have smartphones with internet access
- Users are motivated to improve their health
- Health data self-reporting is accurate enough for trends
- University students represent a viable initial user base
