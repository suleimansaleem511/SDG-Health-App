Stakeholder Analysis for VitaTrack
1. Principal Stakeholders (Direct Users) :
    - University Students: The primary target market who will utilize the application to handle the stress, sleep habits, and physical conditioning.
    - Young Adults: People who need to create a healthy lifestyle and develop positive habits in a long-term perspective.
2. Secondary Stakeholders (Indirect Users/Beneficiaries) : 
    - Medical Professionals: Doctors or experts who might be the recipients of health information exported by users to assist them or advise them better on their medical conditions.
    - University Faculty, Taylor: The PSE module lecturers and reviewers which serve as the University Faculty, that is, the so-called clients of the project and evaluate the success of the project and its compliance with the Software Engineering standards.
3. Internal Stakeholders (The Project Team) : 
   - Group Leader / Scrum Master ( Mohammad Suleiman Saleem ): This role is involved in documentation and Trello management, as well as project coordination.
   - Requirements / Analysis Lead: The person will be in charge of the requirements gathering and modelling.
   - UI/UX Designer ( MaryBindhu Suresh Amrrish Tobin ): He will work on the visual interface, wild-frames, and mockups.
   - Development & Testing Lead: Lead in charge of construction of the prototype and evaluation.
